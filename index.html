CREDA BEM Monitoring

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the CREDA BEM Monitoring mobile application ("the App") collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our services. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected.

1. Information We Collect

To provide efficient monitoring and asset inspection services, the App collects the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and user account credentials provided during registration or login.
  • Location Data: Precise GPS location coordinates.
  • Media Files: Photographs captured directly through the device camera.
  • Asset Data: Inspection details, metrics, and technical information related to the monitored assets.

2. Purpose and Use of Data

The collected data is used exclusively for official monitoring, verification, and reporting purposes, including:

  • Verifying the physical presence and status of assets.
  • Generating analytical reports and dashboards for CREDA administration.
  • Ensuring transparency and accountability in field operations.
Important Notice on Permissions:
• GPS Location: Location data is collected only while the App is actively being used for monitoring activities to mark asset coordinates accurately. We do not track your location in the background.
• Camera Access: Camera access is required solely to capture real-time monitoring photographs as evidence of inspection.

3. Data Security & Storage

We implement robust technical and organizational security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Data is securely transmitted over encrypted channels (HTTPS) and stored on authorized secure servers.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data is only accessible to authorized CREDA personnel and administrators for official verification and evaluation purposes.

5. Children's Privacy

This App is designed for institutional and professional use by authorized personnel and does not target or knowingly collect data from individuals under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
