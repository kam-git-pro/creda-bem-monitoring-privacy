# Privacy Policy – CREDA BEM Monitoring Last Updated: 18 June 2026 CREDA BEM Monitoring ("the App") is developed for monitoring and management of renewable energy assets under CREDA programs. ## Information We Collect The App may collect and process: * User account information * Name and email address * GPS location during monitoring activities * Photographs captured as monitoring evidence * Asset inspection data * Device and application diagnostic information ## How We Use Information Collected information is used for: * Asset monitoring and reporting * Verification of field inspections * Performance analytics * Compliance tracking * System administration and support ## Location Data The App collects GPS coordinates when monitoring activities are performed. Location information is used solely for verification of field visits and monitoring records. ## Camera and Photos The App requires camera access to capture monitoring evidence. Photos submitted through the App are stored securely and linked to monitoring records. ## Data Storage Monitoring records, photographs, and related metadata may be stored using secure cloud infrastructure including Supabase and Cloudflare R2 storage services. ## Data Sharing Information is shared only with authorized CREDA personnel, monitoring agencies, and government officials involved in monitoring and maintenance activities. We do not sell user data. ## Data Security Reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards are implemented to protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. ## User Rights Users may request correction of inaccurate information through their organization administrator. ## Retention Monitoring records and associated evidence may be retained for administrative, audit, compliance, and reporting purposes. ## Changes to this Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updated versions will be published on this page. ## Contact For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, contact: CREDA BEM Monitoring Team Email: bemetara.creda@gmail.com
